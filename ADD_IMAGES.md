## ğŸ” Troubleshooting

### Image not showing?
1. **Check filename** - Must be exact (case-sensitive on some systems)
2. **Check location** - Must be in `public/images/hero/` or `public/images/gallery/`

- **Target**: Under 500KB for hero, under 300KB for gallery
